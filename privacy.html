Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Chrome Extension provided by us ("we", "our", or "the extension"). This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect user data.

1. Information We Collect

This extension may collect the following data, only when necessary to function:

  • Stored user preferences (using Chrome Storage API)
  • Tab information (when using activeTab permission)

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII).

2. How We Use Your Information

Any data collected is used strictly to provide the extension's core functionality. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Any settings or preferences are stored locally using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or ad networks.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to ensure any local data handled by the extension is protected within the Chrome environment.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
